Students
  • The UNC Center for Career Readiness is hosting the Spring 2025 Job, Internship, and Graduate School Fair on Thursday, Feb. 15, from 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. in the University Center Ballrooms. Employers will be recruiting for full-time jobs, part-time jobs, internships, and Graduate Schools will be providing information on their programs. Admission is free and students and alumni from all majors are encouraged to attend! Register to attend here.
  • Are you thinking about studying abroad? The deadline to apply for Summer and Fall 2025 study abroad programs is Wednesday, Feb. 28. For more information and to apply, contact the Education Abroad team at abroad@unco.edu or visit their web page.
Faculty and Staff
  • Help UNC celebrate its most outstanding staff members by attending the 53rd Annual Staff Recognition Celebration on Thursday, March 7, at 1:00 p.m. in the University Center Ballrooms. The event will feature recognition of service milestones, a presentation of awards, and more. Register to attend.
